The Bookmarklet

The bookmarklet will inject the Annotator into a webpage and send your annotations to annotateit.org for safe keeping. To get started simply click the link below to load the bookmarklet and try it out. Refresh the page and load the bookmarklet again to retrieve your annotations from the server.

Click to run the bookmarklet!

How do I use it? We think you'll get the hang of it pretty quickly. Once it's loaded try it out by selecting some text and clicking on the note icon. Enter a note and press “Enter ↩”: you should see your annotation as a highlighted piece of text. Hover over the highlight with your mouse to view, edit and delete the annotation.

What happens to my annotations? The ones you create on this page will be stored on annotateit under a demo account. Soon you'll be able to create your own account and generate your very own bookmarklet.

Where can I find out more? Head over to annotateit.org to find out more about the bookmarklet. If you're interested in how it works or want to give us some feedback you can do so on by visiting the Annotator project on GitHub
